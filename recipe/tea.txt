This is a simple Tea/Chai Recipe in Kenya

1. Heat together in a 3 to 4 quart saucepan or commonly sufuria:
    1 cup of water
    1 1/2 tsp tea leaves(1 tea bag)

2. Boil 10 minutes then Add:
    1 cup of milk

3. Heat near boiling the Add to taste:
    2 to 4 teaspoons sugar

Enjoy you tea the kenyan style.
